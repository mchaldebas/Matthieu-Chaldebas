Project Showcase

5ULTRA: A Machine-Learning Tool for Variant Prioritization

Problem

Genetic researchers frequently face the challenge of interpreting 5'UTR variants. While significant, they are often overlooked because existing tools lacked a reliable scoring method for prioritizing candidates.

Action

To address this critical gap, I developed 5ULTRA, a comprehensive method using Python and a Random Forest model.

Result

  • High Accuracy: Achieved a high ROC-AUC of 0.79 and strong correlation (œÅ = 0.56) with experimental pQTL data.
  • Enabled Novel Discoveries: Successfully applied to explain variants in cancer, GWAS, and rare infectious diseases.
