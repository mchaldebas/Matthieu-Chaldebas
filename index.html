Project Showcase

5ULTRA: A Machine-Learning Tool for Variant Prioritization

Problem

Genetic researchers frequently face the challenge of interpreting 5'UTR variants. While significant, they are often overlooked because existing tools lacked the scope and a reliable scoring method for prioritizing candidates.

Action

To address this critical gap, I developed 5ULTRA, a comprehensive method using Python and a Random Forest model. The pipeline:

  • Systematically annotates SNVs, indels, and splicing variants.
  • Integrates the most recent and comprehensive uORF databases.
  • Implements a novel, validated machine-learning score to rank variants.

Result

  • High Accuracy: Achieved a high ROC-AUC of 0.79 and a strong correlation (œÅ = 0.56) with experimental pQTL data.
  • Enabled Novel Discoveries: Successfully applied to explain variants in cancer (COSMIC), common traits (GWAS), and rare infectious diseases.
